# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Research AI Lab is committed to creating an open, respectful, and intellectually serious community.

We want this project to be welcoming to researchers, students, educators, technologists, librarians, and contributors from different disciplines, countries, and levels of experience.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and generous in discussion
- Critique ideas with care and clarity
- Cite sources when making substantive claims
- Welcome questions from newcomers
- Disclose uncertainty when uncertainty exists
- Prioritize shared learning over status performance

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or intimidation
- Personal attacks
- Dismissive or exclusionary behavior
- Deliberate misinformation
- Plagiarism or misrepresentation of sources
- Using AI outputs deceptively or without appropriate disclosure where disclosure is required

## Community Norms

Research AI Lab values:

- Intellectual honesty
- Methodological rigor
- Curiosity
- Humility
- Openness
- Accountability

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions or discussions that violate these standards. Repeated or severe violations may result in temporary or permanent exclusion from project spaces.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies across repository discussions, issues, pull requests, community spaces, events, and other official project channels.
