# RAL-002: Mission, Vision and Strategic Goals

## Mission

To develop open, rigorous, and reproducible frameworks for AI-assisted scientific research that help individuals and institutions conduct better inquiry.

## Vision

To become a trusted open reference architecture for responsible AI-assisted research across disciplines, beginning in Spanish-speaking ecosystems and extending globally through reusable methods, labs, and public learning resources.

## Strategic Objectives

- Build a coherent public methodology for AI-assisted research
- Create reusable toolkit components that improve research quality
- Develop exemplary domain labs that demonstrate the method in practice
- Grow an open community around rigorous research workflows
- Prepare the architecture for future agentic and operating-system layers

## Five-Year Vision

Within five years, Research AI Lab should function as:

- A recognized methodological reference
- A multi-lab open knowledge initiative
- A public learning environment for researchers and students
- A foundation for auditable research agents
- A bridge between open science culture and practical AI workflows

## Impact Goals

- Improve the quality of AI-assisted research practice
- Lower the barrier to disciplined research workflows
- Create accessible methodological infrastructure in Spanish and beyond
- Strengthen reproducibility, citation quality, and critical analysis
- Encourage responsible adoption of AI in research environments

## Success Metrics

Success should be measured through a mix of quality, adoption, and ecosystem indicators:

- Number and quality of published labs
- Reuse of toolkit components
- Contributor diversity and retention
- External references, forks, and adaptations
- Evidence of educational and institutional use
- Community trust in the standards and architecture
