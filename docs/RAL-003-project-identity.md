# RAL-003: Project Identity

## Brand

Research AI Lab

## Positioning

Research AI Lab is an open methodological initiative for AI-assisted scientific research. It sits at the intersection of open science, research design, evidence synthesis, and applied AI workflows.

## Target Audience

- Researchers
- Graduate students
- Undergraduate students
- Educators
- Policy analysts
- Think tanks
- Open-source contributors
- Research-adjacent technologists

## Value Proposition

Research AI Lab helps people do better research with AI by offering a durable architecture of methods, labs, toolkit components, and quality standards rather than isolated tool tips.

## Tagline

Open Research. Open Science. Open Intelligence.

## Voice and Tone

The voice should be:

- Precise
- Warm
- Serious
- Accessible
- Ambitious
- Calm

The tone should avoid hype while still conveying momentum and possibility.

## Brand Personality

- Scholarly, not academic in a closed sense
- Open, not vague
- Technically aware, not tool-obsessed
- Visionary, not grandiose
- Structured, not bureaucratic

## Core Messages

- Better tools require better methods
- AI should strengthen research standards, not weaken them
- Open methodological infrastructure can improve scientific work at scale
- Research quality depends on transparency, reproducibility, and judgment

## Elevator Pitch

Research AI Lab is an open initiative building the methodological infrastructure for AI-assisted scientific research. Instead of focusing on a single tool or model, it creates durable frameworks, reusable labs, and evidence-driven workflows that help researchers work faster without sacrificing rigor.
