# RAL-009: Research AI Toolkit Specification

## Purpose

The Research AI Toolkit is the official set of reusable assets that support the Research AI Lab methodology.

It is not a random collection of prompts. It is a structured toolkit aligned with research stages.

## Official Components

### Workflow Cheat Sheet

A compact map of the full research process from question framing to final synthesis.

### Research Canvas

A one-page framework to define the question, significance, assumptions, and scope of a study.

### Evidence Matrix

A structured table for comparing sources, findings, methods, data, and limitations.

### Prompt Library

Curated prompts grouped by research phase, with clear intended use and caution notes.

### Search Operators

Reference patterns for academic databases, search engines, and literature discovery workflows.

### NotebookLM Template

A suggested structure for organizing source collections, notes, and synthesized outputs.

### Critical Reading Guide

A checklist or framework for reading sources with attention to methods, data, claims, and limitations.

### Research Planning Canvas

A planning layer for timelines, milestones, dependencies, and expected outputs.

### Research Checklist

A quality-control list for the full workflow.

### Writing Checklist

A checklist for clarity, citation integrity, argument logic, and responsible AI use in writing.

### Publication Checklist

A final review layer before publication or dissemination.

## Design Requirements

Toolkit assets should be:

- Reusable
- Printable or easy to adapt
- Understandable without extensive onboarding
- Aligned with documented methodology
- Tool-agnostic where possible

## Governance

Official toolkit components should be versioned and updated carefully because they influence downstream labs and community practice.
